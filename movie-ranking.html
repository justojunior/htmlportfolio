The Greatest Movies According to Justo

My top three movies of all time

Prison Break

This is my favourite movie of all time. I just love how the plot of the story was created and the insane characters. Michael Scoffield, the main character, has a detailed tattooed plan of how to break his innocent brother,Lincoln Burrows out of prison. My favourite part of the movie is the "chicken foot" challenge.

Money Heist

The Professor, the main character, assembles a team of crazy individuals to carry out a crazy heist at the bank of Spain.

Queen of the South

This is a tale of a young orphan lady, Teresa, who rose to become one of the biggest drug dealers in Sinaloa and America at large against all the odds.
